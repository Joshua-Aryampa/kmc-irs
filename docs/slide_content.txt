Introduction | Web-based Incident Reporting System (IRS) for Kiira Motors Corporation / Digitizes incident reports from submission through verification to approval / Built as a Django application with Keycloak single sign-on for plant employees / Goal: one system usable plant-wide, with a clear audit trail and accountable sign-offs / This presentation summarizes the problem, solution, challenges, progress, and next steps

SLIDE 3
3. The Problem | Incident reporting was manual, slow, and difficult to track across the plant / Limited visibility into open incidents, late submissions, and workflow bottlenecks / Hard to produce consistent closed reports with signatures and audit history

5. The Challenges | Integrating Keycloak (realm, client, redirect URI, service account for search) / Replacing automatic routing with flexible employee search and manual sign-off selection / Server/client clock sync for JWT validation during login / Signature hosting still to be finalised
